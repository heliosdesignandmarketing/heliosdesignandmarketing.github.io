Facebook Chatbot

This programme will help customers on your business's Facebook Page without needing your input. It is the perfect way to create an extra sales funnel, as we will see below, and free up time for more important tasks within the business.

DEMO

How it works


As seen in the image above an initial message is sent with a few different options for the customer to choose. This then creates a flow of different routes and these can be as simple or as complicated as they need to be. The software can answer all basic questions about the business and more importantly can create a sales funnel that results in the customer booking an appointment. Below we will go through the flow that results in a booking.

The Start

Our software is used to build out a flow one message at a time. The "First Name" tag will change based on who the software is messaging. The potential customer then chooses an option in response to the question which leads to an answer or even more questions, as seen above. Images as seen more clearly in gallery below.

Sales Funnel

The Sales Funnel truly starts at the services block. So we use the other 3 options to funnel the customer to the services block regardless of whether they chose it or nor. Images as seen more clearly in gallery below.

Services Block

Now we can display all our products/services and the potential customers are one step closer to becoming actual customers. Images as seen more clearly in gallery below.

Final Stage

Finally the Facebook user is urged to make a phone therefore completing the sales funnel without needing the attention of a present employee or the owner. Images as seen more clearly in gallery below.

Please note, the following demo is very basic and the complexity of each chatbot will differ from company to company. The demo page being used is a made up car wash. A car wash was only chosen for simplicity as it illustrates the perks of the Chatbot perfectly without making things too complicated.

Software (without customisation) only responds to messages but for the purpose of this demo the FB page messages first.
A reply option is chosen and this results in a follow up message. We always make the bots with the intention of getting them to take action so we push them in the required direction.
Now the user receives the same message they would have if they clicked the services option since this is one that moves them down the sales funnel.
Finally the call to action is sent and the customer completes/moves onto the next stage of the sales funnel.
This is how we create the chatbot and we do so with the intention of getting every option to the same end point.
These 3 options result in urging the customer to continue on to the services.
When at the services all options will move them closer to making a purchse.
Here they are given the call to action that will either complete the sales funnel or move them even further along it depending on how complicated purchasing from your business is.
