How a website can help a business

In order to maximise the benefits of a website we need to understand how your business operates and there are 3 main things to consider when creating a website: Visibility, Creating Action and the End Result

Visibility

We can build the best website in the world but if no one is using it, it is completely pointless. Here at Helios we love to work with businesses that already have a marketing system in place, this can be anything from billboard ads and TV ads to social media and word of mouth, but this is not essential as long as you are willing to invest in a marketing system at some point. A marketing system has to already exist or is something that you are willing to create otherwise Helios and your business are not compatable as we will not make a website that yield no results.

Creating Action

A website cannot just look pretty it has to achieve something and should result in potential customers taking action of some kind. With an understanding of how your business gains new customer and retains old ones we can tailor the site to your business's needs.

End Result

A great website will allow a business to do many things such as: increase sales, increase the quality of leads and customers, target new niches and improve the company image (these are just to name a few). A site can also solve a number of problems for a business such as: saving time on telling customers basic info like location and opening hours, reducing reptitive tasks (such as having a contact form that collects customers info easily) and reducing the number of bad/incompatible customers straight away.

It is our job to ask you all the correct questions so we can make a website specific to your business and maximise the rewards this site will give you. So give us an email and we can arrange a call to start planning your site.
